My name is Ryan Escoffery, and I am originally from Jamaica. Growing up, I developed a strong appreciation for technology and how it shapes the way people live, work, and communicate. Coming from a vibrant and resourceful culture, I learned the importance of adaptability, creativity, and persistence—qualities that continue to guide me in my personal and academic journey.

I chose to enroll in the Computer Systems and Information Technology class because I am passionate about understanding how computer systems operate and how technology can be used to solve real-world problems. In today’s digital age, having strong IT skills is essential, and I want to build a solid foundation that will allow me to stay competitive and innovative. This course provides me with the opportunity to develop technical knowledge, improve problem-solving abilities, and gain hands-on experience with systems that are critical in many industries.

My professional goals are to build a successful career in the field of information technology, where I can contribute to developing efficient, secure, and user-friendly systems. I am particularly interested in expanding my skills in areas such as network management, cybersecurity, and system administration. Ultimately, I aspire to work in a role where I can continuously learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact, while also creating opportunities to give back to my community and inspire others to pursue careers in technology.
